DIGITAL PAINTING

This project explores the integration of typography and visual design in soap packaging. Using a modified typeface inspired by sea foam, the design emphasizes typography, with 70% of the layout featuring the custom typeface for a strong brand identity. Complementary sea-inspired colors, patterns, and textures enhance the theme, while a subordinate font ensures readability for product details. This project showcases the synergy between type, visuals, and functionality in impactful packaging.

This typographic poster is a celebration of Helvetica, a font known for its clarity, neutrality, and versatility.
